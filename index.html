ELEPHANT

Elephants are the biggest terrestrial animals. They are found mostly in dense forests and are always found in groups. The elephants are black in colour with a small tail, two big ears, thick legs, a trunk, and two little eyes. Elephants eat twigs, leaves, fruits, etc. and are herbivores. Elephants are the strongest and most intelligent animals.

Fox

Foxes are small-to-medium-sized omnivorous mammals belonging to several genera of the family Canidae. They have a flattened skull; upright, triangular ears; a pointed, slightly upturned snout; and a long, bushy tail. Twelve species belong to the monophyletic "true fox" group of genus Vulpes.
